Hellfire Memorial

Overview

Hellfire Memorial is a custom competitive format designed to pay homage to Hellfire's long history at MirageCraft. The main constraint of this format is the fact that all teams must include, exclusively, Pokémon previously used by Hellfire members (or deeply connected) in competitive play (gyms, tournaments, etc.).

Battle Clauses

Form Clause

A player cannot have two Pokémon sharing a form on a team, unless specified in the "Allowed Pokémon" observations. A player may use multiple Pokémon with the same National Pokédex number on a team, as long as their forms differ.

Example: A player may have both a regular Braviary and a Braviary-Hisui on their team, but may not have two Starmie.

Endless Battle Clause

Players cannot intentionally prevent an opponent from being able to end the game without forfeiting.

Example: A Slowbro can create an endless battle by having a Leppa Berry equipped and the moves Recycle, Heal Pulse, Slack Off and Block. You are not allowed to trap an opposing Pokémon and force an infinite battle by healing the opponent after PP stalling it.
